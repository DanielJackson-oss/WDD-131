BudgetJar

Watch your spending fill up, not spiral out.

BudgetJar is a simple way to keep your spending in check without spreadsheets, logins, or clutter. Set a total budget, log each expense as you spend it, and watch the jar fill in real time so you always know exactly how much you have left. It's built for students and anyone working with a tight, month-to-month budget who wants a fast, no-fuss way to stay on track.
